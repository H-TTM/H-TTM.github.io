CARYS & DUNCAN

About the day

After 3,746,880 minutes / 2602 days / 7 years, 1 month, 16 days of being together we have decided to get married, on Saturday 8th June 2019.

We would love for you to make it to this day to celebrate us becoming husband and wife.

The wedding is being held in Oxwich, Gower, Swansea, as we met and lived in Swansea together for 4 years and love the Gower and all it's many beaches.

Further details about this day can be found either on this website or on the wedding invite.

Event Venue

Penrice Castle Estate, Gower

The wedding is being held on the grounds of Penrice Estate, Gower. You will need to park at the top of the field
the easiest way to find the location is to copy this into Google Maps - 51.578904, -4.173466

There will be sign posts to show you where to enter the location and where to park.


There is no parking available at the church so a shuttle bus service will be provided. This will
pick you up from the parking location and drop you back after the ceremony.
